The Evolution of Knance.com

  • 2018-2020

    Our Modest Beginnings

    Knance.com started as a small tech review blog, focusing on the latest innovations in the electronics industry.

  • March 2021

    A New Dawn

    In early 2021, we transitioned into a full-fledged e-commerce platform, offering high-end electronics to discerning customers worldwide.

  • December 2022

    Global Recognition

    By the end of 2022, Knance.com was recognized as a leader in online electronics sales, known for our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction.

  • July 2024

    Innovating for the Future

    Our recent launch of a secure, anonymous payment gateway using Bitcoin has further solidified our position as an industry innovator.
